Your safety, first

Sterilized from every angle

Your piercing is performed by a certified female piercing artist trained in aseptic technique. Every tool passes through four layers of sterilization before it touches your skin.

Autoclave

High-pressure steam sterilization for every reusable instrument, cycle-logged after each use.

Ultrasonic Cleaner

Sound-wave agitation removes debris from every groove before instruments are sterilized.

Dry Heat Sterilizer

Sustained high heat for tools and jewelry that shouldn't meet moisture.

UV Sterilizer

Ultraviolet exposure keeps prepped jewelry and tools contamination-free until use.

"Respect the skin. Sterilize within."
